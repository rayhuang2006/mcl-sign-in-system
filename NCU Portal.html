    1. New students, please first activate your account at https://ncu.edu.tw/q/freshman-account-activation .
    2. Students, please login with your Portal SPARC account and password. Forget password, change your password at https://ncu.edu.tw/q/email-account-change-password, and then try again.
    3. For new faculty and staff, the portal account will be active at noon. If you have any problem with your account (the account is not email format), contact #57771. If you forget your password, use the Forgot Password function above.
    4. For student, if you forgot your password, please refer to: http://wiki.cc.ncu.edu.tw/wiki/Portal_Q2
    5. For Alumni Login, please enter your ID card number. For default password, please enter 7 digits of your birth year, month and day. (ex. 0881001)
